MTA (Medial Temporal lobe Atrophy) scale (Recommended on Coronal T1W images):

The score is based on a visual rating of the followings:

(1) The width of the choroid fissure,      (2) The width of the temporal horn,      (3) The height of the hippocampal formation

Score 0: No atrophy

Score 1: Only widening of choroid fissure

Score 2: Also widening of temporal horn of lateral ventricle

Score 3: Moderate loss of hippocampal volume (Decrease in height)

Score 4: Severe volume loss of hippocampus

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Fazekas scale (White matter T2FLAIR hyperintensity lesions) (Only on Axial T2FLAIR images):

Score 0: None or a single punctate lesion

Score 1: Multiple punctate lesions

Score 2: Beginning confluency of lesions (bridging)

Score 3: Large confluent lesions
